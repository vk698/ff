Cancellation and Refund Policy

We understand that sometimes users may need to cancel their registration.

1. Cancellation

Users can cancel their registration within 24 hours of payment. After this period, no cancellations are allowed.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds will only be issued if the tournament is canceled by the organizer. Processing time for refunds is 5-7 business days.

For refund requests, contact support@example.com.
